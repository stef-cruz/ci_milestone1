Call & Collect

The books you love now available to you through the
new ‘Call and Collect’ service at Dublin City Libraries.

How the Call & Collect service works

Pick your book. Choose any from the catalogue.

If you are interested in a particular author or book, have a look at the catalogue to see if the items you want are on shelf.

Let us know what you want. We can hold the book, CD or DVD for you.

Tell us what books you are interested in through this form and we'll do our best to get them for you.

Pick up your books and enjoy! Respecting social distancing, of course.

Library staff will then organise a date and time for you to come to your chosen call-and-collect library to pick up your books.
